About



I believe in thinking empathetically and leading humbly. Strong processes, transparent communication and goal oriented execution are the three pillars I value for high-performing teams.

I am a Program Manager and Agilist with 9+ years of experience. I have worked with diverse teams across different geographies (US, Germany, Malaysia, India, Nepal) in projects ranging from e-commerce, marketplace, customer support to brand engagement and corporate marketing. In my experience of building 5 teams, right from hiring, coaching, process setup and management, taught me a lot about people, talent and stakeholder management. I know when to look at the bigger picture and when to give attention to details. As a servant leader, my focus is to utilize the proven frameworks to enhance team performance and keep them on critical paths of the projects.

I have worn many hats, including that of a developer, technical manager, project manager, program manager, social media manager and agile coach. I believe we all have unique talent which thrives in healthy cultures. On the technical front, I have worked hands-on with SQL, web and cloud technologies (AWS, PHP, Python, jQuery, Apache). I am an amateur business analyst and well versed with basic business intelligence model including data collection, integration, analysis and visualization.

Beyond work, you can find me spending time in reading, in gym, doing yoga and listening to podcasts. I enjoy the experiences that allow me to push my limits, including solo travels, scuba diving, meeting diverse people and learn different cultures.
